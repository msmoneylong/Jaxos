# Understand Prompt Pack Access

Your purchased Prompt Pack tier determines the modules and prompts included with your access. You should not expect prompts outside your tier to be available.

## Prompt Packs and JaxOS subscriptions

Prompt Pack access is different from a full JaxOS subscription. A Prompt Pack may provide access to the Vault without unlocking every JaxOS tool.

## If your access does not look right

First, confirm that you are signed in with the email associated with the purchase and that the correct workspace or account context is open. Before contacting support, gather:

- The email used for the purchase or invitation.
- The name of the Prompt Pack.
- The tier or included content you expected.
- A description of the modules or prompts you can see.

Do not include payment details or other sensitive information in a support request unless support specifically asks for it.
