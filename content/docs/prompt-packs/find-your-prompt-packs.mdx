# Find Your Prompt Packs

Prompt Packs are available from the Vault. Sign in with the email associated with your purchase or access, then look for the pack you want to use.

## Find a pack

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Sign in to JaxOS with the email associated with your purchase or access.

    **Expected result:** You can review the areas available to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the Vault.

    **Expected result:** You can view the Prompt Packs available to you.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Identify the available or purchased pack you want to use.

    **Expected result:** You can distinguish the pack you need from other available content.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the correct pack.

    **Expected result:** You can review the modules and prompts included with your access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If your pack is not available

### The pack is not visible

Confirm that you are signed in with the email associated with the completed purchase. If the pack is still not reflected, contact support.

### The wrong tier appears

Review the modules and prompts shown in the pack. If they do not match the tier you purchased, gather your purchase details and contact support.

### You are signed in to a different email or workspace

Sign in with the email associated with your purchase, then confirm the correct workspace or account context is open.
