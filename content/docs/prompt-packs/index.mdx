# Prompt Packs

A Prompt Pack is a guided collection of prompts organized into modules. Customers can purchase a Prompt Pack and access it from the Vault. A pack may include different access tiers, so the modules and prompts you see depend on your purchased tier.

Opening an available pack lets you work through its prompts. Prompts may ask for business or campaign details before generating an output, which you can review and refine.

## Common questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Where do I find a purchased Prompt Pack?">
    Sign in with the email associated with your purchase and open the Vault. Available or purchased packs should appear there.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can I see only some modules or prompts?">
    A Prompt Pack can have access tiers. You should see the modules and prompts included with your purchased tier.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does a Prompt Pack unlock all JaxOS tools?">
    No. Prompt Pack access is separate from full JaxOS subscription access and may provide access to the Vault without unlocking every JaxOS tool.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
