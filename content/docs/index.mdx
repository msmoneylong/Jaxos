# Welcome to the JaxOS Help Center

JaxOS is an AI-powered workspace for planning, creating, reviewing, and managing marketing content. The tools and navigation you see can vary by your account type, permissions, and workspace.

Start with the section that matches what you need:

- [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started) for access, workspace orientation, and your first actions.
- [Prompt Packs](/docs/prompt-packs) to find and work through purchased Prompt Packs in the Vault.
- [Billing and Credits](/docs/billing-and-credits) to understand eligible AI-generation credits and billing-related information.

If you cannot find the area or access you expect, review the relevant guide before contacting support.
