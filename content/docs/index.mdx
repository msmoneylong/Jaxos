# Welcome to the JaxOS Help Center

JaxOS is an AI-powered workspace for planning, creating, reviewing, and managing marketing content. The tools and navigation you see can vary by your account type, permissions, and workspace.

Start with the section that matches what you need:

- [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started) for access, workspace orientation, and your first actions.
- [Using JaxOS](/docs/using-jaxos) for navigation, workspace context, and locked-feature guidance.
- [Prompt Packs](/docs/prompt-packs) to find and work through purchased Prompt Packs in the Vault.
- [Creative Studio](/docs/creative-studio) to create and review available marketing content.
- [Content Calendar](/docs/content-calendar) to review planned or scheduled content over time.
- [Content](/docs/content) to review available content and respond to approval requests.
- [Reports](/docs/reports) to review available marketing results, summaries, plans, or performance information.
- [Billing and Credits](/docs/billing-and-credits) to understand eligible AI-generation credits and billing-related information.
- [Account and Access](/docs/account-and-access) for sign-in, invitations, and account-access guidance.
- [Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting) for safe next steps when something is missing, locked, or not completing.
- [Frequently Asked Questions](/docs/frequently-asked-questions) for concise answers to broad JaxOS questions.

If you cannot find the area or access you expect, review the relevant guide or [Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting) before using the [support option available to you](/docs/contact-support).
