# Understand Content Calendar Statuses

Content Calendar can show a status on each visible item. Status labels and their availability can vary by account and item.

<Callout type="info">
A calendar item’s visible status is the source of truth for that item.
</Callout>

## Review the visible status

Open the item when more detail is available, then rely on the status and information currently shown in JaxOS. Do not assume a workflow rule or next action from a status alone.
