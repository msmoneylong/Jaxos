# Content Calendar

Content Calendar helps you review planned or scheduled content over time. The items, views, and actions you see can vary by account type and permissions.

You may be able to review content by date, status, client, workspace, or other available filters. Use the controls currently shown in your account.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why can’t I see expected calendar content?">
    Confirm the current workspace or client context, the selected date range, and any available filters. Calendar visibility can vary by account and permissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change a calendar item from the calendar?">
    Use only the actions shown for your account. Available calendar actions can vary.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which status should I rely on?">
    Rely on the status currently shown on the calendar item. It reflects the item’s visible state in JaxOS.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
