# Invitations and Workspace Access

An invitation may connect your account to a workspace or client relationship. To view that access, sign in with the email associated with the invitation.

The access you see depends on the invitation and the permissions assigned to it. An invitation does not necessarily provide every JaxOS tool, workspace, client, or action.

## What to check

1. Sign in with the email that received the invitation.
2. Confirm the workspace or client context currently shown, if one is available.
3. Review the navigation and tools visible to your account.
4. If the expected access does not appear, gather the invited email, workspace or client name, and a description of the missing access before contacting support.
