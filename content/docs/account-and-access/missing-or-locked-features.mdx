# Missing or Locked Features

## Symptom

You cannot find a feature you expect, or a visible feature is locked.

## Likely causes

- You signed in with a different email from the one associated with the relevant purchase, invitation, or account.
- A different workspace or client context is open.
- The feature is not included with your current access.
- Your permissions differ from another user's permissions.
- A purchase is not associated with the signed-in account.
- The feature is not yet available to your account.

## Resolution steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Confirm the email used to sign in.

    **Expected result:** You know whether it is the email associated with the access you expect.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the current workspace or client context, if one is shown.

    **Expected result:** You are checking access in the intended context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the navigation and access indicators currently shown.

    **Expected result:** You can identify whether the feature is visible, locked, or unavailable to the current account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Compare the access you expect with the purchase, invitation, or workspace access associated with your account.

    **Expected result:** You have the relevant details to describe the difference if you need help.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Information to gather

- The feature name and whether it is missing or locked.
- The email used to sign in.
- The workspace or client context shown, if applicable.
- The purchase, invitation, or access you expected.
- A screenshot with sensitive information removed, if useful.

## When to contact support

Contact support when you have confirmed the signed-in email and context but expected access still does not appear. Share only the non-sensitive details needed to describe the issue.
