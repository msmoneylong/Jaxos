# Account and Access

Your JaxOS access is connected to the account you use to sign in. Use the email associated with your purchase, invitation, or account so JaxOS can show the access available to you.

Account access and workspace access are related but different. Your account lets you sign in; your workspace or client context determines the tools and information you can see. Access may come from an invitation, a Prompt Pack purchase, a client relationship, a subscription, or an assigned workspace.

## Common questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see my purchase?">
    Confirm that you signed in with the email associated with the purchase and that the correct workspace or account context is open. A purchase may grant only specific access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why do I see fewer tools than another user?">
    Tool visibility depends on account type, purchase, invitation, permissions, and assigned workspace. Different users may have different access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use another email address?">
    To see existing access, sign in with the email associated with your purchase, invitation, or account. If you need help with a different email, contact support with the relevant non-sensitive details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is a feature visible but locked?">
    The feature may require different access or permission. Review the current workspace or client context and the navigation available to your account.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
