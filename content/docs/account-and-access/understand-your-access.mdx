# Understand Your Access

JaxOS access varies by account. The tools and actions visible after you sign in are the practical source of truth for what you can use.

## Types of access

### Prompt Pack buyer access

A Prompt Pack purchase can provide access to available Prompt Packs in the Vault. It does not necessarily unlock the full JaxOS platform or every tool.

### Invited user access

An invitation may connect you to a workspace or client relationship. The tools and information shown depend on the permissions assigned to that access.

### Client access

Client users may have access to tools and information related to their client context. Available actions depend on the permissions shown for that account.

### Agency access

Agency users may have access to additional client or workspace contexts. The navigation and tools available to an agency user can still vary by permissions.

### Broader JaxOS platform access

A subscription or other platform access may provide more JaxOS tools, but it does not guarantee access to every workspace, client, feature, or action.

<Callout type="info">
A purchase or invitation may grant specific access only. Review the navigation and options visible in your current context before assuming a feature is available.
</Callout>
