# Sign In to JaxOS

Sign in with the email associated with your purchase, invitation, or JaxOS account. The sign-in method shown on the page may vary; complete the method JaxOS presents.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open the JaxOS sign-in page.

    **Expected result:** JaxOS shows the sign-in options available to you.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter the email associated with your purchase, invitation, or account.

    **Expected result:** You can continue with the sign-in method shown by JaxOS.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Complete the sign-in method shown.

    **Expected result:** JaxOS opens the account and context available to you.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm that the expected workspace or tools appear.

    **Expected result:** You can identify the navigation and access currently available to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Check the account email and context if access looks incorrect.

    **Expected result:** You can confirm whether you are using the account and workspace associated with the access you expect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If access does not look right

### The email is not recognized

Check that you entered the email associated with your purchase, invitation, or account. Use only the sign-in options JaxOS presents.

### An expected purchase is not visible

Confirm the signed-in email and the current workspace or account context. A purchase may provide only specific access.

### An invitation is linked to another address

Sign in with the email that received the invitation. If you cannot use that email, contact support with the relevant non-sensitive details.

### You signed in to the wrong account

Use the account controls available to you to return to the sign-in experience, then sign in with the email associated with the access you need.
