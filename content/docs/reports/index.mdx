# Reports

Reports help you review available marketing results, summaries, plans, or performance information. Report availability and contents vary by account, service, and workspace.

Always review the report title, displayed reporting period, source, and labels. They are the source of truth for the report you are viewing.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why can’t I see a report I expected?">
    Report availability varies by account, service, and workspace. Confirm the current context and review the Reports area available to you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which dates apply to a report?">
    Use the reporting period displayed in the report. It is the source of truth for the information shown.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I compare two reports directly?">
    Compare reports only after checking their reporting periods, titles, labels, and context. Different periods may not be directly comparable.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
