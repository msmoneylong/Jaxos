# Understand Reporting Periods

The displayed date range is the source of truth for a report’s reporting period.

## Use date ranges in context

- Check the start and end dates before interpreting the information.
- Review comparison periods when they are shown.
- Treat partial periods carefully because they may not represent a full period.
- Do not compare numbers from different periods without considering their dates and context.
