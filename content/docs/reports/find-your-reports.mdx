# Find Your Reports

Use this guide to locate the reports available to your account, service, and workspace.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **Reports**.

    **Expected result:** The Reports area available to your account opens.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the correct workspace or client context.

    **Expected result:** You are viewing reports for the intended context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose an available report or reporting period.

    **Expected result:** The selected report opens or its available information is shown.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the title, date range, and subject before reviewing results.

    **Expected result:** You know the context for the information displayed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
