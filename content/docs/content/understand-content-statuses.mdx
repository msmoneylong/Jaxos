# Understand Content Statuses

Content can show a visible status that describes the item’s current customer-facing state. Status labels and their availability can vary by account and item.

## Review the status shown on the item

Use the status currently displayed in JaxOS as the source of truth. A status does not by itself confirm which actions are available to you.
