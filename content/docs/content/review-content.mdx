# Review Content

Review an item fully before choosing an action. Available filters and actions can vary by your permissions and the item’s status.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **Content**.

    **Expected result:** You can see the content items available to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the correct workspace or client context.

    **Expected result:** You are reviewing content for the intended context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Filter or locate the content item.

    **Expected result:** The item you need is visible.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the item.

    **Expected result:** The item details shown in JaxOS are available for review.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the content, media, date, destination, and notes shown.

    **Expected result:** You have the information needed to decide whether to use an available action.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose an available action only after completing the review.

    **Expected result:** JaxOS applies the action you selected when it is available to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
