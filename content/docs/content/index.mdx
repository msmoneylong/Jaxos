# Content

Content is where you review items available to your account. Some items may require approval, and the actions you can use depend on your permissions and the item’s visible status.

## Guides

- [Review content](/docs/content/review-content)
- [Respond to an approval request](/docs/content/respond-to-an-approval-request)
- [Request changes](/docs/content/request-changes)
- [Understand content statuses](/docs/content/understand-content-statuses)

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why can’t I take an approval action?">
    Approval actions depend on your permissions and the item’s current status. Use the options shown for your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What should I review before responding?">
    Review the content, media, date, destination, and notes shown for the item before choosing an available action.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I ask for a revision?">
    Use the available option for requesting changes and provide specific feedback about the item.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
