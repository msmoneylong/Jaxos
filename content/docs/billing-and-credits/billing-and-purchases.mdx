# Understand Billing and Purchases

Prompt Pack purchases and JaxOS subscriptions are distinct types of access. Do not assume that buying a Prompt Pack unlocks the entire JaxOS platform.

## Where to find billing information

Open the Billing area if it is available to your account. Billing-related information may also appear with your purchase or account records.

## What determines your purchase details

Final charges, renewal terms, and receipts are determined by your actual checkout and account records. Review those records for the details that apply to your purchase or subscription.
