# Billing and Credits

Credits support eligible AI-generation actions in JaxOS. Billing, purchases, subscriptions, and Prompt Pack access may appear separately, and what you can view depends on your account type.

Use the information shown in your account as the source of truth for your available balance, recent usage, and billing-related details.

## Common questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Do all JaxOS actions use credits?">
    No. Credits are used for eligible AI-generation actions, so not every action necessarily uses credits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see my credit balance?">
    Credit visibility can vary by account type. Open the Billing, Usage, or credit area available to your account, if one is shown.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does buying a Prompt Pack include a full JaxOS subscription?">
    No. Prompt Pack purchases and JaxOS subscriptions are distinct types of access.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
