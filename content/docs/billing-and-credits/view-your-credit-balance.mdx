# View Your Credit Balance

Credit balance and usage visibility can vary by account type. Use the area available to your account to review the current information shown in JaxOS.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Sign in to JaxOS.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the Billing, Usage, or credit area available to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the displayed balance.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review recent usage when it is available.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the correct workspace if the balance appears unexpected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If your balance does not look right

### The balance is not visible

Credit visibility may vary by account type. Review the Billing, Usage, or credit area available to you. If no balance is shown and you expected one, contact support.

### A recent purchase is not reflected

Confirm that you are signed in with the correct email and workspace or account context. If the balance still does not reflect what you expect, contact support.

### The wrong workspace is open

Confirm the current workspace or account context before relying on a displayed balance.

### You have a usage question

Review any recent usage information shown in your account. The information shown in the app is the source of truth; contact support if you need help understanding it.
