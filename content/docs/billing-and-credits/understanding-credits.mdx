# Understand Credits

Credits are used for eligible AI-generation actions in JaxOS. Your available balance may include plan credits, purchased credits, or bonus credits.

Not every action necessarily uses credits. The balance and usage information shown in the app is the source of truth for your account. Exact credit deductions can vary and are not listed here.

## What to check

- Your displayed available credit balance.
- Any balance categories shown for your account.
- Recent usage information, when it is available.
