# Frequently Asked Questions

These answers cover broad JaxOS questions. For detailed steps, follow the linked guide for your situation.

## Access and accounts

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why does my navigation look different from another user's?">
    Tools and navigation can vary by account type, purchase, invitation, permissions, and workspace. [Understand your access](/docs/account-and-access/understand-your-access).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see the workspace or client I expected?">
    Confirm the email used to sign in and the current context. A workspace or client may not be available to the current account. [Troubleshoot sign-in and access](/docs/troubleshooting/sign-in-and-access).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is a feature locked?">
    A locked feature may require different access or permission. It does not necessarily indicate a problem. [Troubleshoot locked or unavailable features](/docs/troubleshooting/locked-or-unavailable-features).
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Prompt Packs

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Where do I find a purchased Prompt Pack?">
    Sign in with the purchase email and open the Vault to review the Prompt Packs available to you. [Find your Prompt Packs](/docs/prompt-packs/find-your-prompt-packs).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does buying a Prompt Pack unlock all of JaxOS?">
    No. Prompt Pack access may provide access to the Vault without unlocking the full JaxOS platform. [Understand Prompt Pack access](/docs/prompt-packs/prompt-pack-access).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can I see only some modules or prompts?">
    Available modules and prompts can depend on the Prompt Pack tier associated with your access. [Understand Prompt Pack access](/docs/prompt-packs/prompt-pack-access).
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Credits and billing

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What are credits used for?">
    Credits support eligible AI-generation actions. Not every JaxOS action necessarily uses credits. [Understand credits](/docs/billing-and-credits/understanding-credits).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why does my balance look different than expected?">
    Confirm the signed-in account, current context, and visible balance or usage information. [Troubleshoot missing or unexpected credits](/docs/troubleshooting/missing-or-unexpected-credits).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are Prompt Pack purchases and subscriptions the same?">
    No. They are distinct types of access. [Understand billing and purchases](/docs/billing-and-credits/billing-and-purchases).
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Workspaces and features

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="How do I know which workspace I am using?">
    Review the workspace or client context currently shown in JaxOS. If a selector is available, confirm its selection before you start work. [Understand workspaces](/docs/using-jaxos/understand-workspaces).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is content missing after I switch workspaces?">
    Different contexts can show different information. Check the selected context, date range, and available filters. [Troubleshoot missing content or workspace items](/docs/troubleshooting/missing-content-or-workspace-items).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does every account include Creative Studio, Content Calendar, Content, and Reports?">
    No. Tool visibility depends on the access and workspace available to your account. [Navigate JaxOS](/docs/using-jaxos/navigate-jaxos).
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Content and reports

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I approve or edit an item?">
    Available actions depend on your permissions and the item's visible status. [Review content](/docs/content/review-content).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does a visible status mean?">
    Use the status currently shown in JaxOS as the source of truth for that item. [Understand content statuses](/docs/content/understand-content-statuses).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why are report dates or results different from another report?">
    Check the report title, displayed period, labels, and context before comparing reports. [Understand reporting periods](/docs/reports/understand-reporting-periods).
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Support

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What should I include in a support request?">
    Include the account email, affected context, page or feature, expected result, actual result, steps, approximate time, visible message, and a safe screenshot. [Prepare a support request](/docs/troubleshooting/prepare-a-support-request).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What information should I never send?">
    Never send passwords, sign-in codes, full payment card details, secret keys or tokens, or unrelated private client information. [Prepare a support request](/docs/troubleshooting/prepare-a-support-request).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Should I purchase or submit again when something is missing?">
    No. Check the visible result, account email, and context first. Avoid repeated purchases or submissions while the result is uncertain. [Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting).
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
