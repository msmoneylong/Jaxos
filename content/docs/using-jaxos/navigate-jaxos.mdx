# Navigate JaxOS

Use the navigation available to your account to move between JaxOS tools. The areas and controls you see can vary by your access and current context.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Sign in to JaxOS.

    **Expected result:** The navigation and account areas available to you are shown.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the primary navigation.

    **Expected result:** You can identify available areas such as Vault, Creative Studio, Content Calendar, Content, Reports, or Billing and Credits when they are included with your access.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the current workspace or client context, if one is shown.

    **Expected result:** You know which context the visible information relates to.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the section needed for your current task.

    **Expected result:** The selected area shows the tools or information available in the current context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review any visible status, filters, or access indicators before taking action.

    **Expected result:** You can work from the information currently shown in JaxOS.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Return to the main navigation when moving to another workflow.

    **Expected result:** You can choose the next available area for your task.
  </Step>
</Steps>
