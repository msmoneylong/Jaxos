# Switch Workspaces or Clients

Some customers can choose from more than one workspace or client context. If JaxOS shows a workspace or client selector for your account, use it to change context. A selector is not available to every customer.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Locate the available workspace or client selector.

    **Expected result:** You can see the context currently selected for your account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the selector.

    **Expected result:** The contexts available to your account are shown.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose the intended workspace or client.

    **Expected result:** JaxOS begins showing the selected context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Wait for the visible context to update.

    **Expected result:** The navigation, content, or information reflects the selected context where applicable.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the selected context before continuing.

    **Expected result:** You are ready to work in the intended workspace or client context.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If the context does not look right

### The selector is not shown

Your account may have one available context, or switching may not be available with your access. Use the context currently shown in JaxOS.

### An expected client is not listed

Confirm that you signed in with the email associated with the relevant access. If the client still is not listed, gather the client name and the access you expected before contacting support.

### Navigation changes after switching

Navigation can change because available tools depend on the selected context and your permissions. Use the navigation shown after the change.

### Content appears missing in one workspace

Confirm the selected context, visible filters, and date range where relevant. Different workspaces can show different information.
