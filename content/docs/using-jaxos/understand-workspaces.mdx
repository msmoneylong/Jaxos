# Understand Workspaces

A workspace is the JaxOS context that contains the tools, content, clients, or information available to you. What you see in a workspace depends on the access assigned to your account.

Two users can see different content or navigation because they have different permissions, relationships, purchases, invitations, or workspace access. Confirm the current context before creating content, reviewing an item, or reading a report.

## Know the difference

### Account

Your account is the identity you use to sign in to JaxOS. Its email should match the email associated with your purchase, invitation, or account.

### Workspace

A workspace is the context that determines the tools and information visible to you after signing in.

### Client

A client is a person or organization receiving agency services. If a client context is shown, confirm it before working with client-related content or reports.

### Prompt Pack access

Prompt Pack access is access to available Prompt Packs in the Vault. It is separate from broader JaxOS platform access and does not by itself confirm access to a workspace or every tool.

<Callout type="info">
Before you create, review, or report on content, confirm the workspace or client context currently shown in JaxOS.
</Callout>
