# Using JaxOS

JaxOS is an AI-powered workspace for planning, creating, reviewing, and managing marketing content. The tools available to you depend on your account type, purchase, invitation, permissions, and assigned workspace.

Before starting work, confirm the workspace or client context currently shown in JaxOS. It helps make sure you are reviewing, creating, or reporting on the intended information.

## How the available sections work together

- **Vault** is where eligible users find available Prompt Packs.
- **Creative Studio** provides available content-creation workflows.
- **Content Calendar** shows planned or scheduled content over time when it is available.
- **Content** is where eligible users review available items and any actions shown for them.
- **Reports** contains the marketing results, summaries, plans, or performance information available to the current context.
- **Billing and Credits** provides billing-related and eligible credit information when available.
- Your account menu or available settings provide account-related options.

Not every account includes every section. Use the navigation available to your account as the source of truth.

## Common questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why does my navigation look different?">
    Navigation can vary by account type, purchase, invitation, permissions, and assigned workspace. You may see only the tools available to your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is a feature locked?">
    A locked feature may require a different account type or permission. It does not necessarily mean that something is wrong with JaxOS.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I know which workspace I am using?">
    Review the workspace or client context currently shown in JaxOS. If a selector is available, confirm its current selection before you start work.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does a Prompt Pack unlock every feature?">
    No. Prompt Pack access may provide access to the Vault without unlocking the full JaxOS platform or every tool.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
