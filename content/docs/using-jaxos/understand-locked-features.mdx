# Understand Locked Features

A locked or unavailable feature usually means it is not included with the access currently associated with your account or context. Access can depend on account type, purchase, invitation, role, permission, or assigned workspace.

A Prompt Pack purchase may provide access to the Vault without unlocking the full JaxOS platform. Locked navigation does not necessarily indicate an application error.

## What to check

1. Confirm that you signed in with the email associated with your purchase, invitation, or account.
2. Confirm the current workspace or client context when one is shown.
3. Review the navigation and access indicators currently visible to you.
4. Check whether the feature is included with your available access.

## Before contacting support

Gather the feature name, the email used to sign in, the workspace or client context shown, and a description of what is locked or missing. Do not include payment details or other sensitive information unless support specifically asks for them.
