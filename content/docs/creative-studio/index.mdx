# Creative Studio

Creative Studio helps you create and work with marketing content. Access and available tools can vary by account type and permissions.

You may open Creative Studio from available navigation or arrive there from another JaxOS workflow. Use it to start an available content workflow, provide details, and review the output before taking the next action.

## What you can do

- Open an available content workflow or content type.
- Provide the details requested by that workflow.
- Review the output and use only the actions shown for your account.

## Before you begin

Confirm the correct workspace or client context before creating or reviewing content.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why is Creative Studio unavailable?">
    Access depends on your account type and permissions. Confirm that you are in the correct workspace or client context. If the area is still unavailable, contact support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use every Creative Studio tool?">
    Available creation, editing, generation, download, copy, scheduling, and publishing actions can vary by content type, account, and current release. Use only the actions shown in JaxOS.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Should I publish generated content without reviewing it?">
    No. Review generated content for accuracy and fit before using any available approval, scheduling, or publishing action.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
