# Review Your Creative Studio Output

Review each result before you approve, schedule, publish, or otherwise use it. Generated content is not automatically ready to publish.

## What to check

- Names, dates, offers, links, spelling, tone, and claims.
- The correct brand, client, workspace, and intended audience.
- Any content that needs revision before approval or scheduling.

## Revise before moving forward

Use an available editing or creation workflow to revise content that is incomplete, inaccurate, or unsuitable. After revising, review the updated output again before using any available action.

<Callout type="warn">
Never publish unreviewed generated content automatically. Confirm that the content is accurate and appropriate for the intended brand or client first.
</Callout>
