# Take Your First Steps

Use these steps to orient yourself after you first access JaxOS. The areas you see may vary by account type, permissions, and workspace.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Sign in to JaxOS.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm that the correct workspace or account context is open.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the navigation available to you.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the Vault if you purchased a Prompt Pack.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review available credit or billing information when it is shown for your account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Begin with an available guide or product.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Expected result

You know which workspace or account context you are using and where to find the tools, Prompt Packs, and account information available to you.
