# Understand Your Workspace

Your workspace contains the tools and content available to you in JaxOS. Different users may see different navigation because their account type, permissions, or workspace access differs.

## What you may see

Buyers may primarily use the Vault, Billing, and account areas. Client or agency users may have additional tools. Locked or unavailable features can indicate that the feature requires different access.

## Review your available areas

1. Sign in to JaxOS.
2. Confirm that you are in the correct workspace or account context.
3. Review the navigation and the content available in each area.
4. If an expected feature is unavailable, check whether another workspace or account context applies.

**Expected result:** You can identify the JaxOS tools and content available to your current access.

> **Access difference:** Do not expect every JaxOS user to see the same navigation or features.
