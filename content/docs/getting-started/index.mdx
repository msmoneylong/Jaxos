# Getting Started with JaxOS

JaxOS is an AI-powered workspace for planning, creating, reviewing, and managing marketing content. Your available tools and navigation may differ based on your account type, permissions, and workspace.

Begin by signing in with the account connected to your purchase, invitation, or workspace. Then review the areas available to you. Prompt Pack buyers can start in the Vault; other users can begin with an available guide or product.

## Start here

- [Access JaxOS](/docs/getting-started/accessing-jaxos)
- [Understand your workspace](/docs/getting-started/understanding-your-workspace)
- [Take your first steps](/docs/getting-started/first-steps)

For detailed sign-in and access guidance, see [Account and Access](/docs/account-and-access). For navigation and workspace-context guidance, see [Using JaxOS](/docs/using-jaxos).

## Common questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why does my navigation look different from someone else's?">
    Navigation can vary by account type, permissions, and workspace. You may see only the tools and content available to you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where should I start after buying a Prompt Pack?">
    Sign in with the email associated with your purchase, then open the Vault to look for your available pack.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I cannot see the access I expected?">
    Confirm that you are signed in to the correct email and workspace. If the expected access is still unavailable, contact support.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
