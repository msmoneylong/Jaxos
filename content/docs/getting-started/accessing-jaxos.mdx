# Access JaxOS

Use the email associated with your JaxOS account, purchase, or invitation to sign in. Access may be connected to an invitation, a Prompt Pack purchase, a client relationship, or an assigned workspace.

## Before you begin

- Have access to the email connected to your account, purchase, or invitation.

## Sign in

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open the JaxOS sign-in page.

    **Expected result:** You can enter the email associated with your access.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Sign in using that email and the available sign-in method.

    **Expected result:** JaxOS opens in the account or workspace available to you.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the navigation and confirm that it matches the access you expect.

    **Expected result:** You can identify the tools, content, or Vault access available to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If you cannot sign in or see expected access

Confirm that you used the email associated with the purchase, invitation, or workspace. If you can sign in but cannot see the expected tools or content, confirm the correct workspace or account context is open. If access is still missing, contact support.
