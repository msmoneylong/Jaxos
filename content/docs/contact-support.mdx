# Contact Support

Contact support after you have checked the account email, current workspace or client context, visible status, and relevant troubleshooting guide. Use the support option provided with your JaxOS account or service agreement.

## Check first

- Confirm the email used to sign in.
- Confirm the workspace or client context, if one is shown.
- Review visible messages, status, filters, balance, or purchase information relevant to the issue.
- Avoid repeating a purchase, submission, publish action, or generation while the result is uncertain.

## Include useful details

- Account email.
- Workspace or client name, if applicable.
- Affected page or feature.
- What you expected and what occurred.
- Steps you took.
- Approximate date and time.
- Visible message or status.
- Safe screenshot.
- Purchase or transaction reference when relevant and safe.

<Callout type="warn">
Never send passwords, sign-in codes, full payment card details, secret keys or tokens, or unrelated private client information. Redact email addresses, payment information, and unrelated private details in screenshots.
</Callout>

## Issue categories

- Sign-in or access
- Prompt Pack or purchase
- Credits or billing
- Missing content or workspace
- Locked feature
- Creative Studio or generation issue
- Content Calendar or Content issue
- Reports issue
- Page or action not working
- Other account question
