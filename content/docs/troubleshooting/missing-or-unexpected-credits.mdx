# Troubleshoot Missing or Unexpected Credits

## Symptom

Your credit balance is not visible, a recent credit purchase is not reflected, usage looks unexpected, or the balance differs from what you expected.

## Likely causes

- You are signed in with a different account.
- A different workspace or account context is open.
- Credit visibility varies for the current access.
- The action you are reviewing may not have been eligible to use credits.

## Resolution steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Confirm the email used to sign in.

    **Expected result:** You know which account the displayed balance belongs to.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the current workspace or account context, if one is shown.

    **Expected result:** You are reviewing the balance in the intended context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the Billing, Usage, or credit area available to your account.

    **Expected result:** You can review the visible balance and any available usage information.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review whether the action in question was eligible to use credits.

    **Expected result:** You can compare the action with the information currently shown in JaxOS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to contact support

Contact support when you have confirmed the account and context, reviewed the visible balance and usage, and still need help understanding what is shown.

## Information to gather

- Approximate date and time of the action or purchase.
- The action you took.
- The visible balance and usage information.
- The workspace or account context shown, if applicable.
- A safe screenshot with sensitive information removed.
