# Troubleshooting

Start with the guide that most closely matches what you see. First confirm the email used to sign in and the workspace or client context currently shown. Then read any visible message, status, or access indicator carefully.

Avoid repeating a purchase, submission, publish action, or generation while the result is uncertain. Gather safe details before you contact support.

## Find the right guide

- [Sign-in and access](/docs/troubleshooting/sign-in-and-access) for an account, invitation, workspace, or access issue.
- [Missing Prompt Pack or purchase](/docs/troubleshooting/missing-prompt-pack-or-purchase) when an expected Prompt Pack or tier is not visible.
- [Missing or unexpected credits](/docs/troubleshooting/missing-or-unexpected-credits) when a balance or recent usage does not look right.
- [Missing content or workspace items](/docs/troubleshooting/missing-content-or-workspace-items) for calendar items, content, reports, clients, or workspaces that are not visible.
- [Locked or unavailable features](/docs/troubleshooting/locked-or-unavailable-features) when navigation or a feature is locked or missing.
- [Page not loading or action not completing](/docs/troubleshooting/page-not-loading-or-action-not-completing) when a page, button, submission, or generation does not finish.
- [Prepare a support request](/docs/troubleshooting/prepare-a-support-request) to collect the details that help describe an issue safely.

## Common questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Why does JaxOS look different from another user's account?">
    Access, navigation, tools, and content can vary by account type, purchase, invitation, permissions, and workspace. Review the navigation available to your account. [Understand your access](/docs/account-and-access/understand-your-access).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Should I purchase again if something is missing?">
    No. First confirm the signed-in email, current context, and completed purchase information. Repeating a purchase can make the situation harder to review. [Missing Prompt Pack or purchase](/docs/troubleshooting/missing-prompt-pack-or-purchase).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What information should I gather before contacting support?">
    Gather the affected page or feature, what you expected, what occurred, the approximate time, visible messages, and a safe screenshot. [Prepare a support request](/docs/troubleshooting/prepare-a-support-request).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What should I never include in a support request?">
    Never send passwords, sign-in codes, full payment card details, secret keys or tokens, or unrelated private client information. [Prepare a support request](/docs/troubleshooting/prepare-a-support-request).
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
