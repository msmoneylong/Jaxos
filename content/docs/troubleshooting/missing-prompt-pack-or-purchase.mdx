# Troubleshoot a Missing Prompt Pack or Purchase

## Symptom

An expected Prompt Pack is not visible in the Vault, the expected tier is not available, or a purchase is visible but the related access is not.

## Likely causes

- You signed in with a different email from the one associated with the purchase.
- Another workspace or account context is open.
- The available Prompt Pack access differs from the tier you expected.
- The purchase and the account currently signed in do not match.

## Resolution steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Confirm the email used to sign in.

    **Expected result:** You know whether it is the email associated with the purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Open the Vault.

    **Expected result:** You can review the Prompt Packs available to the current account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm the current workspace or account context, if one is shown.

    **Expected result:** You are checking the intended context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the completed purchase information available to you.

    **Expected result:** You can compare the Prompt Pack name and expected tier with the access shown in JaxOS.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Do not purchase the pack again while the result is uncertain.

    **Expected result:** You avoid creating another purchase while the original issue is being checked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to contact support

Contact support when the email, context, and completed purchase information match but the expected Prompt Pack or access is still missing.

## Information to gather

- Prompt Pack name and expected tier.
- Purchase date.
- Account email used for the purchase and sign-in.
- Safe proof of purchase, with sensitive payment information removed.
- A safe screenshot of the Vault or visible access, if useful.
