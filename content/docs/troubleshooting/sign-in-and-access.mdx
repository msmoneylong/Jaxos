# Troubleshoot Sign-In and Access

## Symptom

You can sign in, but the workspace, client, tools, purchase, or access you expected is not shown. Or you cannot continue with the account you expected to use.

## Likely causes

- You signed in with a different email from the one associated with your purchase, invitation, or account.
- Another account or workspace context is open.
- An invitation is associated with another email.
- The expected workspace is not assigned to the current account.
- The access is not included with the current purchase or permissions.

## Resolution steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Confirm the email used to sign in.

    **Expected result:** You know whether it matches the email associated with the access you expect.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the current workspace or client context, if one is shown.

    **Expected result:** You can confirm the context where JaxOS is showing available information.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the navigation and access indicators visible to your account.

    **Expected result:** You can identify the tools currently available to the account and context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    If an invitation is involved, confirm that you used the email associated with it.

    **Expected result:** You can determine whether the invitation and sign-in account use the same email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to contact support

Contact support when you have confirmed the account email and current context but the expected access is still not visible.

## Information to gather

- The email used to sign in.
- The workspace or client context shown, if applicable.
- The tool, workspace, or access you expected.
- Whether the access came from a purchase, invitation, client relationship, or assigned workspace.
- A safe screenshot with private information removed, if useful.
