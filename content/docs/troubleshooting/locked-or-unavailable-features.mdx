# Troubleshoot Locked or Unavailable Features

## Symptom

Navigation is missing, or a visible feature is locked or unavailable.

## Likely causes

- Available tools depend on your account type, role, purchase, invitation, permissions, and workspace.
- The current workspace does not include the feature.
- A Prompt Pack purchase provides specific access without unlocking the full JaxOS platform.
- Another workspace or client context is open.

## Resolution steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Confirm the email used to sign in and the current workspace or client context.

    **Expected result:** You know which account and context JaxOS is using for the visible navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the navigation and any visible access indicators.

    **Expected result:** You can identify whether the feature is missing or visible but locked.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Compare the feature with the access associated with your purchase, invitation, or workspace.

    **Expected result:** You have the information needed to describe the difference if you need help.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to contact support

Contact support when the account and context are correct but a feature you expected remains missing or locked.

## Information to gather

- Feature name and whether it is missing or locked.
- Account email and workspace or client context shown.
- The purchase, invitation, or access you expected.
- A safe screenshot with private information removed.
