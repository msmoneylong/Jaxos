# Troubleshoot Missing Content or Workspace Items

## Symptom

Expected calendar items, Content items, reports, clients, or workspaces are not visible.

## Likely causes

- A different workspace or client context is open.
- A date range, status, filter, or search is hiding the item.
- The expected item is in another available section.
- The item, report, client, or workspace is not visible to the current access.

## Resolution steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Confirm the current workspace or client context, if one is shown.

    **Expected result:** You are checking the intended context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the available date range, status, filters, and search controls.

    **Expected result:** You can identify controls that may be narrowing the visible items.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Clear filters where that option is available.

    **Expected result:** You can review a broader set of items in the current context.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Check the expected section, such as Content Calendar, Content, or Reports.

    **Expected result:** You can confirm whether the item is visible in the relevant available area.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the item's visible status when you find it.

    **Expected result:** You can use the current status as the source of truth for the item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to contact support

Contact support after confirming the context and visible controls when the expected item, client, workspace, or report is still not shown.

## Information to gather

- Item, client, workspace, or report name.
- The section where you expected to find it.
- Current workspace or client context.
- Selected date range, filters, search terms, and visible status where relevant.
- A safe screenshot with private information removed.
